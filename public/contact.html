Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and products

How long do your wigs last?

Our premium human hair wigs can last 1-2 years with proper care and maintenance. We provide comprehensive care instructions and support to help you maximize the lifespan of your investment.

Do you offer custom color matching?

Yes! We specialize in custom color matching and can create the perfect shade to complement your skin tone. Our consultation process includes color analysis to ensure the best results.

What's included in the installation service?

Our installation service includes custom fitting, professional styling, blending with your natural hair, and a complete aftercare consultation. We ensure a natural, seamless look that enhances your beauty.

Do you provide maintenance services?

Absolutely! We offer ongoing maintenance services including wash and style, retouching, and repair services. Regular maintenance helps ensure your hair always looks its best.
